jollof rice recipe

it is a rich, aromatic, tasty west african one-pot meal. It is similar to jambalaya but with distintive African spices. It consists mainly of cooked rice and tomato stew flavoured with spices such as thyme, scotch bonnet pepper, onions, and garlic.it is also enjoyable with fried plantain

ingredient

  • rice
  • canned tomatoes
  • fresh tomatoes
  • scotch bonnet
  • bell pepper
  • onions
  • garlic
  • ginger
  • seasoning
  • herb(curry powder, black/white pepper,bay leaves, thyme, bouillon powder)
  • ground-nut oil

step for making jollof rice recipe

  1. blend your tomatoes, scotch bonnet, and bell pepper together, chop your onion and keep them aside
  2. parboil your rice and wash. This is to get rid of excess starch in the rice
  3. put your oil in a pot and allow it to heat up
  4. add your chopped onions and allow them to fry
  5. add and fry your blended ingredients for about 10-15 minutes
  6. add your seasoning, curry, thyme, garlic, and ginger
  7. pour your parboiled rice into the pot and stir together
  8. cook on medium heat for at least ten minutes
  9. do not overcook your rice, turn off the heat and allow it to simmer for about five minutes
  10. serve the rice
