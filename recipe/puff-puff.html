puff puff recipe

puff puff

nigerian puff puff is a spongy, deep-fried spherical dough that is originated in nigeria. It is soft and springs back to touch, you know, like when you pinch a foam sponge.

ingredients

  • 250g of plain flour
  • 2 teaspoons of yeast
  • half spoon of ground nut-meg
  • 50g of sugar or to your taste
  • 3 pinches of salt
  • lukewarm water
  • vegetable oil

steps for making puff puff recipe

  1. put the flour in a bowl, add ground nut-meg, powdered yeast, sugar and salt. Mix all this together
  2. add the warm water in small quantities and mix everything by hand till you have a good blend of all the ingredients
  3. keep mixing till the batter is smooth and not too watery to flow from a spoon. It should be thicker than pancake batter
  4. cover the boil with aluminum foil and make sure it is airtight
  5. leave it to rise for about 45 minutes
  6. pour some oil to a deep pot and allow to heat up, or set the temperature to about 170 degree
  7. when the oil is hot enough, scoop some batter into the oil with your hand as much as the spaces in the frying pot will allow
  8. once the other side of the puff puff has turned golden brown, flip to the other side of the ball
  9. when both side are golden brown take out the puff puff and put 'em in a sieve
  10. serve with a chilled drink of your.
